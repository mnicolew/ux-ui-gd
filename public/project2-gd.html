R O L E

Graphic Designer

D U R A T I O N

Two weeks

C O N C E P T

C H A L L E N G E

To refine Velocity Dance Studio's brand identity and provide a new set of guidelines for visual communication. Before the redesign, the modern contemporary dance studio lacked cohesion, consistency, and aesthetic pleasure in its branding.

O U T C O M E

An Elegant and clean-cut brand guide that better reflects how Velocity Dance Studio would like to be perceived. The brand guide consists of client communication goals, logos, icons, color schemes, typography, and a mobile UI flow.
